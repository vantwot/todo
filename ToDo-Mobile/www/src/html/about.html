The ToDo App

The ToDo App is a simple, lightweight, and user-friendly application designed to help you manage your daily tasks effectively. With intuitive controls and a clear, distraction-free interface, the app makes it easy to focus on what's important.

Highlights of The ToDo App include:

  • Easy addition and management of tasks.
  • Editable due dates for each task.
  • Sorting tasks by priority or due date.
  • A sleek, user-friendly interface with light and dark modes.
  • Local storage of tasks for privacy and convenience.
